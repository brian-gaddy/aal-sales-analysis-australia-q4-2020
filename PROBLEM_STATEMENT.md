# Problem Statement

AAL is an Australian apparel brand serving customer groups from kids to seniors across multiple states. The company is pursuing expansion opportunities and needs data-driven guidance for the upcoming year.

The sales and marketing organization must identify the states generating the highest revenue and develop sales programs for lower-revenue states. This project analyzes fourth-quarter 2020 sales data on a state-by-state basis.

## Analytical Scope

The project covers data wrangling, missing-value inspection, normalization concepts, GroupBy analysis, descriptive statistics for Sales and Unit, high- and low-performing states and customer groups, daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly reporting, state and segment visualizations, time-of-day analysis, and executive recommendations.

The original course requirements specify a Jupyter-based report and dashboard-style visualizations for sales and marketing decision support. The portfolio version preserves the notebook and adds a reproducible Python pipeline, interactive dashboard, baseline forecasting, automated tests, and continuous integration.
